Empanadas

Description

Handheld pastries from Latin America and Spain, stuffed with savory or sweet fillings and baked or fried.

Ingredients

  • Dough: Mix flour, salt, butter, warm water. Rest 20 min.
  • Filling: Sauté onion, garlic, ground meat or veggies; season.
  • Assemble: Roll dough, add filling, fold, crimp.
  • Cook: Bake at 200°C for 20-25 min or fry until golden.
